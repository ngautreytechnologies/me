Personal Projects

A small selection of systems projects that reflect the kind of architecture I care about most: integrity, clear boundaries, and software that remains reliable and maintainable as it evolves over time.

CaseCell

CaseCell is a local-first evidence and chain-of-custody platform built for high-stakes workflows, where provenance, auditability, and operational correctness are non-negotiable.

It is designed to produce artefacts that are traceable by construction, with strict separation between trusted custody records and exploratory analysis, ensuring the core remains verifiable regardless of environment or deployment.

Focus: evidence integrity, deterministic systems, provenance, bounded AI support.

DendriFlow

DendriFlow is a separate passion project exploring how developers can be augmented through a modular DevEx toolkit designed to integrate with existing engineering tools and workflows.

It is an R&D space for building composable automation tooling that reduced repetitive overhead, support context retention, and introduce carefully scoped AI assistance only where it is clearly useful and human-controlled.

Focus: developer experience, modular automation, responsible augmentation.
